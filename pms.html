Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Prime Minister Scholarship Scheme, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Must have secured admission in a recognized college or university.
  • Must have achieved a minimum percentage in the qualifying examination.

Benefits

Selected candidates will receive:

  • Financial support for tuition fees.
  • Scholarship amount for books and study materials.
  • Additional support for other academic expenses.

Application Process

To apply for the scholarship, follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit the required documents.
  3. Attend the interview process (if applicable).
